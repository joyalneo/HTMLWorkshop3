OUR PORTFOLIO

We had the privilege of helping some of the best startups and enterprises across multiple industries achieve their business goals.

DAILYAI

Daily AI is a CRM made specifically for lenders. It outperforms existing CRMs by utilizing AI and computer learning to help lenders automate their follow ups, close loans, update client details, etc.

NATIONAL TAXI SERVICES

RideYellow is an innovative ride app from National Taxi Service (NTS). They have partnered with taxi fleets across the US, providing safe and secure rides to people by connecting them with reliable and experienced drivers in their locality at the tap of a button.

PROPERTYOK

PropertyOK is a real estate company launched in 2014 with a predominantly offline business model. They came to us in 2017 to help them transform their online presence. We helped them launch their online real-estate platform. This helped them shift their business to an online model. Now they are one of South India's top real estate platforms.

WAITWHILE

Waitwhile is the world's most intelligent waitlist and scheduling app used by thousands of businesses including Ikea stores, Facebook and LinkedIn. It estimates wait times using predictive analysis which is 30% more effective than humans. They approached us to help the boost their apps performance by helping them migrate from AngularJS to Angular.

LAWLI

Lawli is an intelligent legal assistant providing businesses with low cost document processing services. Powered by AI, Lawli provides law firms and businesses of varying size with the tools to gain control over their legal services and create a positive business environment for everyone.

WRENCH.ai

Wrench.AI helps businesses make better decisions using Big Data-based software and tools. The platform integrates with existing CRMs to boost sales by delivering personalized experiences.
